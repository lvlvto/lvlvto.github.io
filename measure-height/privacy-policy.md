## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Measure Height ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

Measure Height does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All measurements are processed locally on your device and are not sent to any external servers.

## Camera and Sensor Access

The app may request access to your device's camera and motion sensors solely for the purpose of measuring height. This data is processed in real time on your device and is **never** recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Measure Height does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

## Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
